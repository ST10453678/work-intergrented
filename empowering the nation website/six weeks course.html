Six Weeks Course

The Six Weeks Course is designed for individuals looking to gain fundamental skills in a short period. This course focuses on practical training and hands-on experience, enabling participants to quickly acquire essential knowledge and apply it in their daily tasks. Whether you are new to the industry or looking to refresh your skills, this program is perfect for you.

Course Overview

  • Duration: 6 Weeks
  • Schedule: Monday to Friday
  • Course Type: Full-time
  • Location: Empowering the Nation Training Center

What You Will Learn

  • Basic household management skills
  • Gardening essentials
  • Personal hygiene and sanitation
  • Effective communication in the workplace
